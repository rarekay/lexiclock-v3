Welcome to LexiClock

The complete Scrabble companion. Timer, word challenger and training tools — all in one app. Free, offline, works on any device.

Timer + Challenge

Per turn or total time mode. Each tile faces its player. Hit Challenge mid-game to pause the clock and check any word instantly against Collins CSW24 or NWL2023.

Explore

Anagrammer, Q-without-U words, vowel dumps, pattern search and Word of the Day. Everything to help you at the board. All offline.

Train

Daily puzzle, bingo trainer, hook trainer, flashcards and Challenge or Not. Become a better Scrabble player — no internet required.
